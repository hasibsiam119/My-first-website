About Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer, widely considered one of the greatest players of all time. He has won multiple Ballon d'Or awards and numerous league titles with Barcelona and later with PSG and Inter Miami. Messi led Argentina to victory in the 2022 FIFA World Cup, solidifying his legendary status. Known for his dribbling, vision, and goal-scoring, he has broken countless records. Beyond football, Messi is also a global icon and philanthropist.
